About MeSobre Mimאודותיי

I am a physician graduated from Escola Paulista de Medicina - UNIFESP. Between 2021 and 2023, I conducted research at the Department of Nutrition Physiology, focusing on adipose tissue browning, supported by a FAPESP scholarship. In 2023, I served as president of the Sexually Transmitted Infections Combat Service (SCIST - EPM). I have been an active member of the Academic Leagues of Reproduction and Obstetric Assistance and directed several university extension projects, including the Brazilian International Congress for Medical Students (BRAINCOMS) and the Extension Department of the Associação Atlética Acadêmica Pereira Barretto (AAAPB). My clinical experience includes extracurricular internships in Intensive Cardiology at the University of Belgrade and Sexual Health and Reproductive Rights at the Universities of Lisbon and Porto. Projects & ResearchProjetos & Pesquisaפרויקטים ומחקר

  • POSSÍVEL AÇÃO PRÓ BROWNING DO EXTRATO PADRONIZADO DE Ginkgo biloba SOBRE O DEPÓSITO DE TECIDO ADIPOSO BRANCO RETROPERITONEAL DE RATOS COM OBESIDADE INDUZIDA PELA DIETA
    FAPESP Grant (Scientific Initiation) Bolsa FAPESP (Iniciação Científica) מלגת FAPESP (הכשרה מדעית) | ProcessProcessoתהליך: 22/05460-9
    Nov 2022 – Oct 2023

    This research aimed to investigate the anti-obesogenic mechanisms of Ginkgo biloba extract (EGb), specifically evaluating its potential to induce "browning" in white adipose tissue. Using Western Blotting techniques, we analyzed the protein levels of UCP-1, PPARγ, and PGC-1α. The project also included in vitro characterization using the hWA41 immortalized human pre-adipocyte line to observe morphofunctional changes and metabolic remodeling. Esta pesquisa visou investigar os mecanismos anti-obesogênicos do extrato de Ginkgo biloba (EGb), especificamente avaliando seu potencial em induzir o "browning" no tecido adiposo branco. Utilizando técnicas de Western Blotting, analisamos os níveis proteicos de UCP-1, PPARγ e PGC-1α. O projeto também incluiu caracterização in vitro utilizando a linhagem de pré-adipócitos humanos imortalizados hWA41 para observar mudanças morfofuncionais e remodelamento metabólico. מחקר זה נועד לבחון את המנגנונים האנטי-השמנתיים של תמצית Ginkgo biloba (EGb), ובפרט להעריך את יכולתה לעודד "browning" ברקמת שומן לבנה. באמצעות טכניקות Western Blotting ניתחנו את רמות החלבונים UCP-1, PPARγ ו-PGC-1α. הפרויקט כלל גם אפיון in vitro באמצעות קו תאי קדם-אדיפוציטים אנושיים מונצחים hWA41, כדי לבחון שינויים מורפופונקציונליים ושינוי מטבולי.
