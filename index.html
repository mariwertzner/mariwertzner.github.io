About Me

I am a physician graduated from Escola Paulista de Medicina - UNIFESP. Between 2021 and 2023, I conducted research at the Department of Nutrition Physiology, focusing on adipose tissue browning, supported by a FAPESP scholarship. In 2023, I served as president of the Sexually Transmitted Infections Combat Service (SCIST - EPM). I have been an active member of the Academic Leagues of Reproduction and Obstetric Assistance and directed several university extension projects, including the Brazilian International Congress for Medical Students (BRAINCOMS) and the Extension Department of the Associação Atlética Acadêmica Pereira Barretto (AAAPB). My clinical experience includes extracurricular internships in Intensive Cardiology at the University of Belgrade and Sexual Health and Reproductive Rights at the Universities of Lisbon and Porto.
